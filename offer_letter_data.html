Row 4 Start date before acceptance — The candidate's response is dated 2024-04-15 but the start date is 2024-04-10. They were logged as starting 5 days before they officially accepted the offer.
Rows 5 & 6 Duplicate candidate on same role — Maya Patel (maya.patel@email.com) appears twice for Job ID MKT-112 with slightly different dates. Classic double-entry from two recruiters or two systems logging the same candidate.
Row 7 Monthly salary entered as annual — Salary shows $8,500 for a Senior Engineer. Every other role is in the $65k–$115k range. This is almost certainly $8,500/month = $102,000/year — someone entered the monthly figure in the annual field.
Row 8 Job ID vs offer title mismatch — Job ID ENG-205 corresponds to a Software Engineer requisition, but the Offer job title reads "Product Manager." The candidate may be onboarded under the wrong role on paper.
Row 9 Typo in email domain — Email is sarah.jones@gmial.com ("gmial" not "gmail"). The offer was marked Sent but the candidate never received it — it likely bounced silently with no follow-up triggered.
